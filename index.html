Our Story

Crafting

DOM CLOTHINGS was born from a passion for sartorial excellence and a deep reverence for Nigerian fashion heritage. Based in the heart of Festac Town, we bring a refined vision to menswear — honouring tradition while embracing the modern man's needs.

Every garment we create is a labour of love. From the initial consultation to the final fitting, meticulous attention goes into every detail — cut, fabric, embroidery, silhouette.
